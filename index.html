About Us

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Founded in 1994, LHUMB Nagar Academy has been a cornerstone of quality education in Bangladesh for over three decades. Located in Ramganj, Lakshmipur, we have shaped the lives of more than 5,000+ alumni who now serve in every sector of national and international life.

Our institution follows the National Curriculum & Textbook Board (NCTB) syllabus and prepares students for SSC examinations under the Comilla Education Board, consistently achieving among the highest pass rates in the district.

Our Vision

To be Bangladesh's most respected institution, nurturing ethical, intellectually vibrant citizens who drive positive change.

Our Mission

Delivering holistic education through innovative teaching, values-based character development, and community engagement.

Admissions

Join Our School Family

Follow our simple admission process and begin your journey of excellence.

Admission Process

1

Collect Application Form

Visit the school office or download the form from our portal. Forms are available from January 15 onwards.

2

Submit Required Documents

Submit completed form along with all required documents at the school admission office.

3

Admission Test

Appear for the written admission test covering Bangla, English, and Mathematics. Date TBA.

4

Result & Enrollment

Results are published within 7 working days. Selected students complete enrollment with fee payment.

Required Documents

Birth Certificate (original + 2 copies)
Previous school's Transfer Certificate
Last exam mark sheet (original)
4 passport-size photographs
Guardian's NID copy
Admission fee: Govt. Standard
